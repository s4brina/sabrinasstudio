    • Brisbane Rail UI

    In 2022, I created a user interface for a hypothetical service where tax-payers were given the opportunity to decide where the money spent on Brisbane Rail is invested towards. To create the fully functioning prototype, I used Figma, and this can be interacted with below.

    I had to consider a range of elements, such as the accessibility of the interface, how categories were sorted, and the overall flow of the program. I also created a document that explained the process and our design decisions.
